Our Story

IronHand was born from a simple observation: finding reliable, skilled labor shouldn't take days of phone calls and uncertainty. We built a platform that bridges the gap between expert tradespeople and the businesses that need them.

The Mission

To provide a transparent, fast, and secure ecosystem where skill is the only currency that matters. Whether it's an emergency repair or a long-term construction project, we ensure the right hands are on the job.

The Technology

As a project developed with a focus on Information Systems, IronHand utilizes a robust stack to ensure data integrity and real-time performance.
